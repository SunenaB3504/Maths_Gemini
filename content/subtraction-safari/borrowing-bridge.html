What is Borrowing?

When we subtract, sometimes the top digit is smaller than the bottom digit. We can't subtract a bigger number from a smaller one (at least not yet!).

So, we need to borrow (or regroup) from the next place value. It's like asking your neighbor for help!

Borrowing Steps to Remember

  1. 1 Look at the ones column first. Is the top number smaller than the bottom number?
  2. 2 Borrow 1 from the tens place (which is really 10 ones).
  3. 3 Add those 10 ones to the ones digit in the top number.
  4. 4 Subtract the ones column, then the tens column.
  5. 5 Remember to reduce the tens digit by 1 since you borrowed from it.

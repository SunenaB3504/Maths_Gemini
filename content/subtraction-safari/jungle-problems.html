How to Solve Word Problems

Solving subtraction word problems is like finding your way through the jungle. Follow these steps:

1
Read the Problem

Read the entire problem carefully to understand what's happening.

2
Find the Important Information

Look for numbers and what they represent.

3
Determine What's Happening

Is something being taken away? Is something leaving? Is the problem asking for a difference?

4
Write a Number Sentence

Use subtraction to show what's happening.

5
Solve and Check

Solve the problem and make sure your answer makes sense.

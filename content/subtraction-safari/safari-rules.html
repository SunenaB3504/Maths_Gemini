Special Subtraction Rules

1
Subtracting Zero

When you subtract zero from any number, the number stays the same!

7 - 0 = 7
25 - 0 = 25
143 - 0 = 143
🦒 🦒 🦒 🦒

4 giraffes - 0 giraffes = 4 giraffes

If no giraffes leave, we still have the same number!

2
Subtracting the Same Number

When you subtract a number from itself, the answer is always zero!

8 - 8 = 0
42 - 42 = 0
105 - 105 = 0
🦓 🦓 🦓

3 zebras - 3 zebras = 0 zebras

If all zebras leave, we have none left!

3
Order Matters in Subtraction

Unlike addition, the order of numbers in subtraction matters a lot!

10 - 3 = 7
3 - 10 = -7

10 hippos - 3 hippos = 7 hippos

🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛 🦛

3 hippos - 10 hippos = ?

We can't do this yet! We need negative numbers.

4
Subtraction and Addition are Related

Subtraction and addition are opposite operations - they undo each other!

10 - 4 = 6
6 + 4 = 10

Subtraction: 8 snakes - 5 snakes = 3 snakes

Addition: 3 snakes + 5 snakes = 8 snakes

This is why we can check subtraction with addition!

5
Subtracting from Ten

When subtracting from 10, there's a pattern in the answers:

10 - 1 = 9
10 - 2 = 8
10 - 3 = 7
10 - 4 = 6
10 - 5 = 5
10 - 6 = 4
10 - 7 = 3
10 - 8 = 2
10 - 9 = 1
10 - 10 = 0

The pattern: 10 minus a number equals 10's partner to make 10!

Rule Tips

  • 🌟 Look for patterns - Patterns help you solve problems faster and check your work.
  • 🌟 Reverse with addition - If 10 - 6 = 4, then 4 + 6 = 10. Use this to check your answers!
  • 🌟 Remember zero rules - Any number minus zero equals the same number. Any number minus itself equals zero.
  • 🌟 Notice partners of 10 - Learning which numbers add to make 10 helps with mental math.

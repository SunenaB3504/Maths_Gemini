What Are Missing Number Problems?

Sometimes in math, we know the answer but are missing a piece of the problem. We need to figure out what number is hiding!

In subtraction, we might know:

  • The starting number and the answer, but not how much was taken away
  • How much was taken away and the answer, but not the starting number
  • The starting number and how much was taken away, but not the answer

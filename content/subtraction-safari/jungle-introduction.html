What is Subtraction?

Subtraction is when we take away some items from a group.

It's like when monkeys grab some bananas from a tree - the tree has fewer bananas afterward!

🌴
🍌 🍌 🍌 🍌 🍌 🍌 🍌

Tree with 7 bananas

Monkeys take 3 bananas →
🌴
🍌 🍌 🍌 🍌

Tree has 4 bananas left

7 - 3 = 4

Subtraction Symbol

-

This is the subtraction symbol. It looks like a little line or dash.

When you see this symbol, it means "take away" or "find the difference".

8 - 5 = 3
10 - 4 = 6
12 - 7 = 5

Try a Simple Subtraction Problem

There are 9 parrots on a branch. 4 parrots fly away.

How many parrots are left on the branch?

🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜
🦜 🦜 🦜 🦜

We start with 9 parrots.

We subtract 4 parrots that fly away.

9 - 4 = 5

There are 5 parrots left on the branch!

Safari Subtraction Tips

  • 🌟 Start with the bigger number - In subtraction, we always start with the bigger number.
  • 🌟 Count backward - You can count backward to subtract small numbers.
  • 🌟 Use your fingers - Hold up fingers for the first number, then put down fingers for the number you're subtracting.
  • 🌟 Draw pictures - Drawing pictures (like our parrots) can help you solve subtraction problems.

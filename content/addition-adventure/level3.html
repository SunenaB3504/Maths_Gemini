How to Add Two-Digit Numbers

When adding two-digit numbers, remember these simple steps:

  1. First, add the ones (the right-most digits)
  2. Then, add the tens (the left-most digits)
  3. If the ones add up to 10 or more, carry the 1 to the tens

Helpful Tips

  • Always start with the ones place (the right-most digit)
  • Remember to carry when the ones add up to 10 or more
  • Think of it as: First add the ones, then add the tens
  • Check your work: Make sure your answer makes sense

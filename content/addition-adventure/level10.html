Tips for 4-Digit Missing Digit Problems

  • Pay attention to place values: Remember the position of thousands, hundreds, tens, and ones.
  • Watch for carries: When adding large numbers, carries affect other digits and can make these problems tricky.
  • Work systematically: Start by finding what the complete number should be, then identify the missing digit.
  • Verify your answer: Always check your solution by doing the complete addition with your answer in place.

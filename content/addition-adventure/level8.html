Four-Digit Addition with Multiple Carries

When adding large numbers, we often need to carry multiple times across different columns. Here's how to handle it:

  1. Line up the numbers carefully by place value
  2. Start from the right (ones place) and work left column by column
  3. Add each column, carrying when needed
  4. Keep track of all carries as you move left
  5. Double-check your work
Example: Adding 7,865 + 4,978

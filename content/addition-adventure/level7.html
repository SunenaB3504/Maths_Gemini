Understanding Four-Digit Numbers

Four-digit numbers have values in the thousands place!

Thousands Hundreds Tens Ones
3 5 4 2
3000 500 40 2

The number 3542 = 3000 + 500 + 40 + 2

Adding Four-Digit Numbers

We follow the same steps as with smaller numbers, but now we have an extra place value to consider:

  1. Line up the numbers by place value (ones under ones, tens under tens, etc.)
  2. Start from the right (ones place) and add each column
  3. If a sum is 10 or more, carry to the next place value
  4. Continue until you've added all columns

Tips for Four-Digit Addition

  • Careful alignment: Make sure you line up the digits correctly by place value.
  • Keep track of carries: Write down your carries to avoid mistakes.
  • Check your answer: Estimate first (round to nearest thousand) to make sure your answer makes sense.
  • Break it down: Add one column at a time, from right to left.
  • Take your time: There's no need to rush - accuracy is more important than speed!
